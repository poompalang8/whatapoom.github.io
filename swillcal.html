SwillCal

Smart calendar and planning app

SwillCal helps users organize schedules, track important events, and manage daily plans with a clean, mobile-first experience.

What It Solves

  • Missed appointments and deadline conflicts
  • Hard-to-read schedules in busy weeks
  • Slow daily planning workflows

Key Features

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly calendar views
  • Color-coded events and reminders
  • Task planning with lightweight interactions
  • Fast mobile-friendly navigation

Tech Direction

  • UI/UX focused design system
  • Responsive front-end architecture
  • Scalable feature roadmap for future modules
