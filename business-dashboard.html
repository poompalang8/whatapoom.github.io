Business Dashboard

Analytics and KPI tracking workspace

Business Dashboard brings real-time metrics, trend analysis, and team performance snapshots into a single executive-friendly view.

What It Solves

  • Scattered KPI reporting across tools
  • Slow executive decision-making from outdated data
  • Manual compilation of weekly performance updates

Key Features

  • Live KPI tiles with alerts and thresholds
  • Revenue, funnel, and retention dashboards
  • Customizable filters by region and team
  • Shareable performance snapshots

Tech Direction

  • Data visualization-first layout system
  • Component-driven charts and insights panels
  • Optimized for large datasets and fast refresh
