05 / UX Strategy

Decision Logic

The UX strategy focused on lowering analysis friction, clarifying what changed, and helping users move from insight to action without getting lost in data density.

The Problem

Important metrics were scattered

Executives and teams needed to compare KPIs across tools, which slowed reporting, buried thresholds, and made trend reading inconsistent.

Solution

Prioritized signal over volume

The interface pushed critical KPIs forward, structured filters carefully, and made trend changes easier to see in fewer steps.

User Journey

Scan

Open the dashboard and identify the highest-priority change within seconds.

User Journey

Compare

Move between filters, teams, or regions without losing the main context.

User Journey

Decide

Use trend and threshold feedback to act on risks or opportunities faster.

Key UX Principles

Clarity

Hierarchy, contrast of importance, stable filtering, and minimal cognitive overhead for KPI-heavy screens.
